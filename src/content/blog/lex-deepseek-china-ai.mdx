## The DeepSeek Moment

### What Happened
- DeepSeek R1 released with impressive capabilities
- Similar performance to OpenAI models at lower cost
- Open weights model with visible chain-of-thought reasoning
- Shook the AI world and stock markets

### Why It Matters
- Demonstrates China's AI capabilities
- Questions about compute efficiency
- Implications for AI cost curves
- Open source vs closed source debate

## AI Model Comparison

### DeepSeek R1 vs OpenAI O3 Mini
- Similar benchmark performance
- R1 is cheaper
- R1 shows full reasoning chain
- O3 mini only shows summary
- R1 is open weight, O3 is not

### Best Models for Different Tasks
- Claude Sonnet 3.5: Best for programming
- O1 Pro: Good for tricky brainstorming
- More models coming from US and China
