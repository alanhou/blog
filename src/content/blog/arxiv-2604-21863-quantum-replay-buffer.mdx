## Problem

Quantum circuit compilation faces unique challenges:
- Exponential search space for gate ordering
- Hardware-specific constraints (connectivity, calibration)
- Trade-offs between circuit depth and gate count
- Limited quantum hardware access for training

## Replay-Buffer Engineering

Adapting classical RL techniques for quantum optimization:

### Key Innovation

Apply prioritized experience replay to quantum circuit synthesis:

1. **Quantum State Encoding**
   - Represent circuit as sequence of operations
   - Encode circuit properties (depth, fidelity, gate count)
   - Track quantum resource usage

2. **Prioritized Sampling**
   - Priority based on circuit improvement potential
   - Balance exploration vs. exploitation
   - Diverse sampling for better generalization

3. **Buffer Management**
   - Novelty-based prioritization
   - Diversity maintenance in buffer
   - Efficient storage for circuit representations

### Results

| Metric | Standard RL | Replay-Buffer RL |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| Circuit Depth | 124 gates | 87 gates |
| Training Samples | 50K | 12K |
| Convergence Speed | 8 hours | 2 hours |
| Fidelity | 92% | 97% |

## Takeaways

- RL techniques transfer well from classical to quantum domains
- Prioritized replay accelerates quantum circuit learning
- Sample efficiency is crucial given quantum hardware scarcity
- Buffer engineering matters for quantum RL

## 问题

量子电路编译面临独特挑战：
- 门排序的指数级搜索空间
- 硬件特定约束（连接性、校准）
- 电路深度与门数之间的权衡
- 训练用量子硬件访问受限

## 重放缓冲区工程

将经典RL技术应用于量子优化：

### 关键创新

将优先经验重放应用于量子电路综合：

1. **量子态编码**
   - 将电路表示为操作序列
   - 编码电路属性（深度、保真度、门数）
   - 跟踪量子资源使用

2. **优先采样**
   - 基于电路改进潜力的优先级
   - 平衡探索与利用
   - 多样性采样以更好泛化

3. **缓冲区管理**
   - 基于新颖性的优先级
   - 缓冲区中的多样性维护
   - 电路表示的高效存储

### 结果

| 指标 | 标准RL | 重放缓冲区RL |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| 电路深度 | 124门 | 87门 |
| 训练样本 | 50K | 12K |
| 收敛速度 | 8小时 | 2小时 |
| 保真度 | 92% | 97% |

## 要点总结

- RL技术从经典领域很好地迁移到量子领域
- 优先重放加速量子电路学习
- 样本效率在量子硬件稀缺情况下至关重要
- 缓冲区工程对量子RL很重要
